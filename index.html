Welcome

I've created this website to help people who support abortion rights better understand, discuss, and defend their views using evidence-based arguments. For now, I prefer to remain anonymous. What I can share is that I hope to study medicine and pursue a career in obstetrics and gynaecology. Through my studies and research, I've become increasingly aware of the challenges and inequalities that exist within healthcare systems around the world, particularly those affecting women and reproductive healthcare.



My goal is to contribute to positive change. In discussions about abortion, I often see conversations become emotional, unproductive, or focused on the wrong issues. This website was created to help people better understand common arguments within the abortion debate and to provide evidence-based responses that encourage constructive, respectful, and informed discussion.

Whether you're looking to strengthen your understanding of reproductive rights, prepare for a debate, or simply learn more about the topic, I hope the resources here help you engage with the issue thoughtfully and effectively.
